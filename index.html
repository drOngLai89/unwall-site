Privacy Policy

Effective date: 16 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Unwall handles information when you use the app.

Unwall may process information you choose to enter in the app, such as reflections, prompts, or text submitted while using the app’s features.

How information is used

Information may be used to provide app features, improve the service, maintain security, troubleshoot technical issues, and respond to support requests.

Third-party services

Unwall may rely on third-party providers for hosting, infrastructure, content processing, analytics, crash reporting, or other technical services needed to operate the app.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be disclosed where required by law, to protect users, or to operate and secure the service.

Data retention

Data is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask questions about your data, request deletion where applicable, or ask about how information is handled.

Children’s privacy

Unwall is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes take effect means the updated policy will apply.
